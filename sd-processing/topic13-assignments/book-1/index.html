Formative Assessment

The details of your formative assessment are listed in this area. The marks you get from this assessment will NOT go towards your final module mark but will act as a learning tool for attempting and submitting an assignment.

Overview of assessment

In the week 2 lab, challenge exercise 3 asked you to develop your own game, animation or art, etc.

We gave you some examples if you were falling short on inspiration.

For this assignment, you can choose to either:

  • Develop your own game/animation/art etc that you started in week 2 OR

  • Use the maze game as a starting point and improve it e.g.

    • Maybe when the mouse has eaten the cheese, notify the user that the game is over and they won.
    • Maybe add a "livesLost" variable? If the mouse hits the wall three times, the game is over and they have lost.
    • Maybe changing the sound when obstacles are hit.
    • any other changes that would improve the game.
